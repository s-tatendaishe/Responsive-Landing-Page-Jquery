2004 Spider-Man 2

Spider-Man 2 is a 2004 superhero film directed by Sam Raimi. It follows Peter Parker's struggles to balance his life as a college student and his responsibilities as Spider-Man. When Dr. Otto Octavius turns into the villain Doc Ock, Spider-Man faces a formidable adversary. The film is praised for its engaging storyline and visual effects.
